ASSET.PRO

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026

Asset.Pro is published by AceStarr Solutions and is built with a local-first approach. We do not require user accounts, we do not run advertising or analytics SDKs, and we do not store your inventory data on AceStarr Solutions servers.

1. Information stored on your device

Asset.Pro stores your inventory information on your device, including item names, descriptions, values, categories, room or location information, and document or photo references you attach inside the app. This information may be included in your normal device backup systems, such as iCloud or Google backup, depending on your device settings.

2. Photos, camera, and media access

If you choose to photograph or attach documentation inside Asset.Pro, the app may request access to your camera or photo library. Media selected or captured in the app is used only for your inventory records and is not uploaded to AceStarr Solutions servers by the app itself.

3. Device security features

Asset.Pro can use device-level biometric authentication or a PIN you configure to help protect access to your records. Security data needed for this feature is stored locally on your device.

4. Sharing, export, and email

Asset.Pro allows you to export reports and share selected records. Exported files and anything you choose to share are created only at your direction. Once you send or store an exported file outside the app, its handling is governed by the destination you chose, such as your email provider, file app, or cloud storage provider.

5. Third-party services

Asset.Pro does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs to profile you or track your behavior. The app relies on standard platform and Expo libraries to provide local storage, camera access, biometric authentication, and sharing capabilities.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your records remain on your device until you edit or delete them, clear the app's data, or remove the app. If your device backups include app data, copies may also remain in those backups until you manage them through your platform provider.

7. Policy updates

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be reflected in the app and in the public policy URL you use for distribution.

8. Contact

For privacy questions about Asset.Pro, use the developer contact published on the app listing or support channel provided by AceStarr Solutions. Before submitting to Google Play, make sure the same contact email shown in your Play Console listing is active and publicly reachable.

Summary

  • No required account: you can use Asset.Pro without creating an account.
  • No server-side inventory storage: your core inventory data stays on your device.
  • No ad tracking: the app does not include advertising or analytics profiling SDKs.
  • User-directed sharing only: exports happen only when you choose to create or share them.
